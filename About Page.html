About Me

Hello! I'm Ricardo Hernández, a bilingual (English/Spanish) engineer with a strong background in data analytics, automation, and process improvement.

Currently, I work at Collins Aerospace as a Data Analytics Engineer II, where I automate data pipelines and deliver actionable insights that influence high-level decision-making and improve reliability strategies. I also have hands-on experience with tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, and Power Automate to streamline processes and enhance efficiency.

Before my current role, I held positions in both general engineering and analytics, consistently using Lean Six Sigma principles to drive results. From creating automated dashboards to eliminating manual workflows, I’ve helped teams improve operational efficiency and reduce errors across engineering and pricing systems.
